About All-The-Things

Everyone has more ideas than time. All-the-things is an app that allows anyone keep track of their ideas, good and bad alike, without a pesky mouse getting in the way of your flow.

How To Use This App

Just start typing, your main idea will be captured as the title to your list. Use tab, enter, space, backspace, and delete to navigate through the simple interface.

The Tech Stack

All-The-Things was built using React and initialized using Create React App.

A Little About Me

As former engineer and public school educator, I have always been drawn to math, physics, and the science and art of creating things. Putting all those pieces together, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering. I enjoyed being an educator at charter school and being a part of a small engineering firm, where I worked in a variety of team roles. I always found myself being pulled towards technology and programming. As my professional experience grew, I began to see how I could leverage my engineering, teaching, and problem solving background in new ways. This lead me to the Turing School of Software and Design where I honed the skills necessary to be part of a dynamic software development team.
